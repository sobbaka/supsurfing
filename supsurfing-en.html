About us

SUP - one paddle rowing, suitable for people of all ages and fitness conditions, easy to learn. From the age of seven, clildren can confidently stand on a supsurf board.

Sup board provides opportunity to stroll on the surface of Tbilisi Sea and enjoy the beauty of the Georgian nature. You will not only experience great impression,but will also spend time with benefits for your health. Supsurfing is a decent workout for your body.

Sup surfing is suitable for calm weather, in light wind we hold rafting.

You are welcome to bring your pets, we are pet friendly ❤️

TBILISI WEATHER

Weather for sup surfing

For sup surfing, as for any other water sports, the weather plays an important role. Strong winds or stormy conditions can make sup surfing unsafe and uncomfortable. In addition, time can affect an individual's mood, which can affect their abilities and performance.

So for the best experience, plan your holiday according to the weather.

One-day Sup tours

We arrange transfer or deliver supboards from Tbilisi to Zhinvali reservoir, Sioni lake, Paravani lake, Tsalkinskoye reservoir, Bazaleti lake. We take care of the transfer from Tbilisi and back.

You will have a ride on the Sup in the most famous and picturesque reservoirs of Georgia. We carry out briefing, issue life jackets and waterproof cases for phones.

All sup tours are booked in advance. To clarify the price, please contact us, as it depends on the number of people and the reservoir you have chosen.
